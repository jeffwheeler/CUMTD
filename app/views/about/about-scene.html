Developing CUMTD
The tracker was developed by Jeff Wheeler at the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign, in just a few days. It was designed for entry into Palm's Student Application Showcase at the ACM Reflections | Projections conference.
CUMTD is the Champaign-Urbana Mass Transit District, which provides bus service the the Champaign-Urbana area, including to the entire UIUC campus.
Of course, the Champaign-Urbana Mass Transit District owns the CUMTD name and logo. Data used in this application is pulled from free and open data sources already published by the CUMTD on the web.
